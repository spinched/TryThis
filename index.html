Gus's Timeline

Where the Little Guy has been until now:

  • March - April 2020

    Kuala Lumpur SPCA

    Gus was born KL, Malaysia and brought to the local SPCA when he was still a little guy. During his time there, his name was either Rio or Berlin. We aren't totally sure since his collar said Rio but they gave us the passport for a cat named Berlin. It doesn't matter though because his name is Gus now.

  • April 16, 2020

    We adopted him!

    The little guy chose us, and who were we to refuse such an angel boy? We brought him home and introduced him to our other cat, Kitty Hawk. She was not very pleased with not being an only child anymore, but I like to gaslight myself into thinking they're besties.

  • April 2020 - June 2022

    Life with us in KL

    Gus Gus loved having us around 24/7 during those 2.5 years of lockdown. When he was still little, he would sleep on our shoulder like a little parrot while we were in online school. He helped keep me sane during my high school years.

  • July 2022

    Moving to Tokyo

    As I headed off for University, the family packed up and moved (along with the two kitties of course) to Tokyo, Japan. I imagine Gus cries everyday because he can't be with me every day and I'm his favorite.

He's so Little

A few days after we got him

Gus started off as just the smallest little kitty boy in the entire world. He had a curly little tail and a big bobble head. As he's gotten older, he's become a lot bigger and is now a chonky little boy. Nothing can stop a kitty who loves his snacks and is always munching. That's how he got to be such a chonker from being the littlest boy in the whole world.

A Good Brother

Gus Gus and Kitty Hawk

Kitty Hawk is our other cat, named after the North Carolinian landmark where the first Wright Brothers flight occured. Gus and Kitty Hawk love to fight and bully each other (like they will literally smack each other with their paws), but they love each other like the other 25% of time. Gus likes to get on Kitty Hawk's moves by getting in her space, and Kitty Hawk will smack him for it.

Pro at Hiding

He's afraid of people

Being a Covid pet, Gus didn't meet anyone outside of my immediate family for two years. This meant that when we did eventually have people come over, he was very confused and afraid. He's a little bit of a coward like that. He also loves to sleep in strange hiding places on occassion.

His Patterning

Not actually just a black cat

Surprise! Gus is actually a torti with melanism. When the sun hits him just right, you can see his stripes and markings. As he's gotten older, he's gotten quite a bit darker as well, but you can still sometimes see his patterning.

He's me in cat form

Gus is me and Kitty Hawk is Kati

It worked out that the relationship between Gus and Kitty Hawk is very similar to the relation between myself and my sister, Kati. We spend most of our time innocently hanging out which quickly turns mean, but, by the end of the day, we're besties again. Gus and Kitty Hawk are the same way.

He loves shows

He's an anime fan

Gus is a big fan of any type of cinema. He enjoys watching us play video games (especially Animal Crossing). He is a big action movie fan and watched anime very closely. I'm not 100% on this, but I"m pretty sure he want to be a Hollywood director when he grows up.
